GNAN Lab @ Georgia Tech

Georgia Tech Network and Mobile Computing Research Group - GNAN

Our research focuses on algorithmic solutions for interdisciplinary problems in the areas of wireless networking and mobile computing. We typically ground the algorithms we develop on sound theory, and at the same time progress the algorithms into implementations in a practical system. Our emphasis is on performing deep research that has clear real-world implications, and to this end we aggressively pursue commercialization of our research.
